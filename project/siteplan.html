🔸 Site Name 🔸

Name : Korea Travel Club
This site introduces places in South Korea for tourist to enjoy visiting
Optional domain : k-travelclub.com

🔸 Site Purpose 🔸

The site provides an information for places to visit in South Korea. The information includes good places to stay, eat, and how to get there and around. It also recommends different courses according to the duration of stay. There is membership information form for exclusive services.

🔸 Scenarios 🔸

  • Where are some good places outside of the capital city to travel?
  • What are some good accomodations around the places?
  • What are some good food that you can eat around each destinations?
  • How do you get around when traveling in the country?
